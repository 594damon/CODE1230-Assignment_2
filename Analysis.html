    MAP |
    These two 3D maps are showing the geological map of Australia and the coloured areas are representing the ratio of the population participating casual and normal work for men and women respectively where the ratio of men is on the left and the women is on the right. Moreover, the excursions show the amount of male or female having jobs.
    So some area will be coloured black where the casual work is greater than the normal work. On the other hand, if there are more normal work and the ratio is greater than 1, these areas will be dyed as pale red. It is clearly that the distribution of participating works of male and female is relatively the same. But the spread in casual and normal work participation for male and female has much difference.




    LATTE LINE |
    The LATTE LINE is an imaginary boundary that separate the Sydney into two parts according the economic from the north western airport to the Parramatta.
    These images shows that around north of CBD and above the latte line the areas are white and pale blue which means there could be more part-time jobs provided for people.
